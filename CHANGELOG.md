## [2.0.0] - 2021-05-18
### Added
- Vault `grant` method - grant TONs without receiving WTON tokens
- Vault `withdraw` method - owner can withdraw TONs from the vault without providing tokens
- Tests for `grant` and `withdraw` functionality
